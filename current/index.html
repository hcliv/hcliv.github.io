Excerpted from the biography of Steve Jobs

We all have a short period of time on this earth,we probably only have the opportunity to do a few things really great and do them well,None of us has any idea how long we are going to be here,nor do I,but my felling is I've got to accomplish these things while I'm young. What drove me? I think most creative people want to express appreciation for being able to take advantage of the work that’s been done by others before us. I didn’t invent the language or mathematics I use. I make little of my own food, none of my own clothes. Everything I do depends on other members of our species and the shoulders that we stand on. And a lot of us want to contribute something back to our species and to add something to the flow. It’s about trying to express something in the only way that most of us know how—because we can’t write Bob Dylan songs or TomStoppard plays. We try to use the talents we do have to express our deep feelings, to show our appreciation of all the contributions that came before us, and to add something to that flow. That’s what has driven me.
